However, many concerns came out right after I became an programmer. I think the only reason why I devote myself into this "world" is that I love mathematics and solving practical problems with it. Just with that "stupid" decision I imporved my skills and mind which would help me step up closer to science.

I think this is exactly what people always talk about, "Find your passion and dive into it." I hope my choice is always the best one for me and will help contribute to make everyone's life beautiful.
